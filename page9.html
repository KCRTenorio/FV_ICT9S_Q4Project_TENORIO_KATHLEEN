Noli Me Tangere

Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Ibarra is a priviliged young man who returned to the Philippines after studying in Europe.
  • He symbolizes the wealthy and liberal European-educated Filipinos.
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is the fiance or childhood sweetheart of Ibarra.
  • She symbolizes the feminine ideal in women back in the days.
Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is an arrogant friar.
  • He symbolizes the corrupt officials in the government.
